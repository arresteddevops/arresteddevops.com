OpsCode Renamed Chef

The big news of the day is that OpsCode, after raising $32MM in Series D, has changed their name to Chef. Because, well, that’s what they are. The Chef people.

That’s cool and all, but the twitters quickly panicked at how challenging it would be to find ANYTHING Chef-related on the Googles.
